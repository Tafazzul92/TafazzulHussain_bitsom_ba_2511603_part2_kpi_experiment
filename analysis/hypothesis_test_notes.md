
# Hypothesis Test Notes

## Null Hypothesis
There is no significant difference in paid conversion rates between Control and Treatment groups.

## Alternate Hypothesis
Treatment group improves paid conversion rate significantly.

## Test Type
Two-tailed t-test

## Significance Level
0.05

## Metric Tested
Paid conversion rate

## Test Result
P-value = 1.0000

## Decision
Fail to Reject Null Hypothesis

## Business Interpretation
The hypothesis test helps determine whether the onboarding campaign should be launched broadly.
